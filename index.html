DSC USTP SOLUTIONS WEEK

Solutions Week is an event that encourages everyone to develop a solution using technologies that Google currently provides. It is also an event in preparation of the annual Solutions Challenge of Google Developer Student Clubs which gives the participants the opportunity to solve a problem that must align to at least one of the 17 Sustainable Developer Goals.
